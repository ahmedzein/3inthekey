The Podcast

Introducing a fresh perspective on basketball through the eyes of passionate Canadian fans. This show goes beyond borders, delivering comprehensive coverage of everything basketball, from the NBA to NCAA, CEBL, USports, and the vibrant world of International Ball. Expect more than just updates – we dive deep into the game, offering in-depth analysis, insightful breakdowns, and captivating discussions. Join Elias and Fuad every Sunday for an engaging live session on TSN 1200 at Noon Eastern. Can't make it live? No worries! Tune in on demand using any Podcast app at your convenience and stay immersed in the exhilarating world of basketball with us.

Elias

Elias, a proud native of Ottawa, Ontario, possesses an immense passion for basketball, particularly for the Lakers. His deep-rooted affection for the sport took hold early in his life and has remained unwavering ever since. With a career as a basketball play-by-play announcer, Elias previously served as the distinctive voice for the Carleton University Ravens Men's and Women's basketball teams. Presently, he shines as the sideline reporter for the Ottawa Blackjacks, all while hosting 3 In The Key, where he brings his love for the game to the forefront of engaging basketball discussions.

Fuad

Fuad embodies the legacy of the GOAT and the unforgettable era of the 90’s Chicago Bulls. Witnessing the sheer greatness of both the team and the timeless phenomenon that is Space Jam, basketball became his lifelong passion. As our go-to social media whiz, he'll be the one jazzing up our social pages regularly. Just a heads-up, though: his heart beats with fervor for the Bulls, so if you spot an extra dose of Bulls love, please pardon him in advance.
